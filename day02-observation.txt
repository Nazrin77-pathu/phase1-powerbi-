Number of rows: 50 rows (plus 1 header row = 51 total lines)

Number of columns: 6 columns

Column meanings:
  - Student_ID : Unique ID for each student (S001 to S050)
  - Name : Student's full name
  - Gender : Male (M) or Female (F)
  - Math_Score : Math test score out of 100
  - Science_Score : Science test score out of 100
  - Attendance : Attendance percentage (out of 100)

One thing that surprised me:
Students with attendance above 90% have an average score of 88 in both subjects, while students with attendance below 70% average only 58. That's a 30-point difference! Attendance seems to predict performance more than gender does.
