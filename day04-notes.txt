Day 04 – Power Query (Shaping Data)

1. Split Column (Join Date):
I split the Join Date column into day, month, and year to make the data more structured and easier to analyze.


2. Filter Rows (Remote Work):
I filtered the dataset to keep only rows where Remote Work is true to focus on employees working from home.


3. Custom Column (Bonus):
I added a Bonus column by multiplying salary by 10% to calculate additional earnings.


4. Custom Column (Salary Category):
I created a Salary Category column using conditions to classify employees as High, Medium, or Low based on their salary.
